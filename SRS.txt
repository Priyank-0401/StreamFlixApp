Final Deliverables  1. PROJECT DESCRIPTION  A Subscription Billing & Revenue Management System that automates recurring billing for SaaS/streaming-style products. It supports subscription creation, automatic billing cycles, taxes & discounts, proration, invoices, credit notes, refunds, dunning with retries, renewal reminders, and revenue analytics (MRR, ARR, churn)  2. SCOPE  In Scope:  - Product & Plan catalog (tiers, add-ons, metered components)  Subscription lifecycle (trial, active, paused, canceled)  - Billing engine (cycle runs, proration, mid-cycle changes)  - Invoices, credit notes, and receipts  - Payment processing (Mock Payment Service - simulated payments)  - Taxes (GST/VAT), discounts, coupons  - Dunning (retry strategies), reminders & notifications  - Revenue analytics dashboard (MRR/ARR, churn, retention)  - Role-based portals: Admin, Finance, Support, Customer  3. 10. WORKFLOWS & SEQUENCE FLOWS

10.1 Subscription Creation

- Select plan and payment method

- Validate coupon/tax

- Create subscription (trial/active)

- If setup fee/immediate charge invoice payment attempt

- On success: receipt; on failure: past_due and retry

10.2 Renewal Billing (Cron)

- Find subscriptions expiring next day

- Generate invoice with proration/discounts/taxes

- Attempt payment (idempotent) and update status



- Send receipt or failure notice

10.3 Upgrade/Downgrade Mid-cycle

- Compute proration credit/debit

- Charge now or defer to next renewal

- Update subscription items; audit log

10.4 Dunning & Retry

- On failure: past_due and schedule retries

- Notify to update payment method

- After max attempts: cancel/on_hold per policy
